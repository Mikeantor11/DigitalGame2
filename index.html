Phone Memory

  • High Concept: Practice you memorization!
  • Player Experience Goals: Work out your brain as you play!
  • Genre: Memorization
  • Game play: The player needs to press down to progress and then put in the same numbers that were on screen when prompted to.
  • Prototype goal: Test how the player will enjoy a game baseed on memorization.
  • Features: The user will get a opportunity to trest their ability to memorize numbers.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting is you are trying to remember the numbers that the stranger's phone has been receiving.
  • Story: You have found a stranger's phone and must remember the numbers that have been called from someone else.
  • Target Audience: Casual players who want to strengthen their brain!
  • Play time: About 2-3 minutes.
  • Strategies: Memorize what is shown!
  • Prototype Assets: I found the phone by googling cell phone clip art.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Any other memorization game.
