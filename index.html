Interview Practice

  • High Concept: Practice taking an interview.
  • Player Experience Goals: Experience a personal debate between wanting to have some fun or being serious.
  • Genre: Interview simulation.
  • Game play: The player needs to select whicheve responses they want! :)
  • Prototype goal: To figure out how the user makes choices in this sort of sense of choosing inbetween the "correct" response or the fun response.
  • Features: The user get's to choose between one of three options, these three options will decide wheither or not they get the job, or are spotted as a demon.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting is you're being interviewed by a bird as a demon, and you're trying to get the job without being spotted.
  • Story: You come from the Underworld trying to seek employment without being spotted as a demon coming from the Underworld.
  • Target Audience: Casual players who want a short fun experience.
  • Play time: About 2-3 minutes.
  • Strategies: Choose the response that is the most fun for you!
  • Prototype Assets: I found the police and the bird suit was found on google.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Similuar to a dating Simulator.
